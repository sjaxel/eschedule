==Overview==
=== Format ===
* Participants:
** Seeded Teams: [[Samsung Galaxy|Samsung]], [[Jin Air Green Wings]], [[NaJin e-mFire]], [[SK Telecom T1]], [[CJ Entus]] and [[KT Rolster]].
** Qualified Teams: [[GE Tigers]] and [[Incredible Miracle]].
* Regular Season:
** 2 x 6 weeks
*** First half - Jan 7th to Feb 14th
*** Second half - Feb 25th to Apr 11th
** Double Round Robin
** All games are Bo3
** Substitutions can done in between games
** Top 4 teams will qualify for the [[2015 LCK Spring/Playoffs|Playoffs]].
&lt;!--
* All bracket stage games are Best of Five, including the Grand Finals
** The final game is Blind Pick while other games are Draft Pick
** Teams losing in the Quarterfinals continue to play in [[NLB Summer 2014#Diamond League|ITENJOY NLB Summer 2014 (DiamondLeague)]] --&gt;
* Special Awards are given to selected players after the season:
** A Season MVP award will be given to one player.
*** After each match, the press will decide the MVP and award points
**** Season: 100 points
**** Playoffs: 300 points
**** Finals: 500 points
** A KDA award will be given to one player of each role.
*** The player of each role (Top, Jungle, Mid, AD Carry, and Support) who has the highest Kill-Death-Assist rate will win.
